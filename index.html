Thesis guardrail — read before Week 1
The VAE is a proposal generator, not a scorer. It samples candidate modification patterns; the deterministic ΔΔG/activity head still decides. Generation proposes, biophysics decides — which is what keeps a generative model inside the framework's "not a language model" stance.
